.. _uk.ons.LC3202WA0010:

Bad or very bad health
---------------------------------------------------------------------------



.. figure:: ../img/uk.ons.LC3202WA0010.png
  :width: 100 %
  :align: center









Measure "Bad or very bad health"  density per sq. kilometer  for one point:


.. code-block:: postgresql





  UPDATE {table_name}
    SET {new_numeric_column} =
      OBS_GetMeasure(
        CDB_LatLng(51.4684455122, -3.18483352661),
        'uk.ons.LC3202WA0010'
      );





Measure "Bad or very bad health" within an area:

.. code-block:: postgresql

    UPDATE {table_name}
      SET {new_numeric_column} =
        OBS_GetMeasure(
          ST_Buffer(CDB_LatLng(51.4684455122, -3.18483352661), 0.01),
          'uk.ons.LC3202WA0010'
        );






Measure "Bad or very bad health" percent of "Population age 3 and over" at one point:

.. code-block:: postgresql

  UPDATE {table_name}

      OBS_GetMeasure(
        CDB_LatLng(51.4684455122, -3.18483352661),
        'uk.ons.LC3202WA0010',
        'denominator'
      );
