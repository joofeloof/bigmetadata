.. _ca.statcan.geo.pr_:

Canada, provinces and territories
---------------------------------------------------------------------------











Obtain "Canada, provinces and territories" geometry at one point:


.. code-block:: postgresql





  INSERT INTO {tablename}(the_geom)
    SELECT OBS_GetBoundary(
      CDB_LatLng(43.6559499126, -79.3796539307),
      'ca.statcan.geo.pr_'
    )





Obtain all "Canada, provinces and territories" geometries within an area:

.. code-block:: postgresql

  INSERT INTO {new_table_name} (the_geom, {geo_id_column})
    SELECT *
    FROM OBS_GetBoundariesByGeometry(
      ST_Buffer(CDB_LatLng(43.6559499126, -79.3796539307), 0.01),
      'ca.statcan.geo.pr_'
    )
